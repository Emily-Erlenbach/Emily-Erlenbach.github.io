  • KIN 448: Exercise and Health Psychology (Spring 2022)

Teaching Assistant
  • KIN 448: Exercise and Health Psychology (semesters)
    Undergraduate and graduate-level course with approximately 50 students covering topics of the epidemiology of physical activity and exercise participation, exercise and mental health, and theory-based exercise interventions and psychological outcomes.
  • KIN 201: Physical Activity Research Methods (semesters)
    Undergraduate-level course with approximately 30 students per section focusing on defining physical activity and associated terms and concepts; providing detailed understanding of approaches and tools for measuring physical activity; and introducing statistical methods for physical activity research.
  • KIN 140: Social Science of Human Movement (semesters)
    Undergraduate-level course with approximately 30 students per section introducing psycho-social aspects of human movement.
